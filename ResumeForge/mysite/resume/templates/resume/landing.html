Frequently Asked Questions

Is ResumeForge completely free?
Yes! ResumeForge is 100% free. No hidden fees, no credit card, no watermarks. All templates and features are available to all users.
Are the templates ATS-friendly?
Absolutely. All 4 templates are designed to pass Applicant Tracking Systems used by companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and global firms. Clean formatting and proper section headings ensure your resume is machine-readable.
Can I create multiple resumes?
Yes. You can create as many resumes as you want — tailored for different jobs, industries, or roles. Duplicate a resume and tweak it in seconds.
How do I download my resume as PDF?
Click the "Download PDF" button from the builder or preview page. Use your browser's built-in "Save as PDF" to get a crisp A4 PDF. No third-party tools needed.
Is my data safe?
Your data is stored securely using Django's best practices with CSRF protection, hashed passwords, and email verification. We never share your data.
